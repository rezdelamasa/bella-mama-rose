Our Story

Authentic Sicilian Family Recipes

Bella Mama Rose takes its name from the well loved Rose Denino Zancocchio, a long time friendly and familiar face of her family’s tavern, Denino’s Pizzeria located in Port Richmond Staten Island. She is known to most as “Aunt Rose” and our pizzas as well as many of our dishes are prepared with her authentic Sicilian family recipes that have been passed through the family’s generations. With Rose’s love of family and friends in mind, we offer most of our menu in family style portions so that you can make yourself at home and enjoy a home cooked meal with yours without all of the mess and work.

Our restaurant has been completely renovated and renovated to a trendy, homey atmosphere with just the right touch of class. The main dining room features an open kitchen faced with a brick wall and comfy booth seating. Off of the main dining room you can make yourself at home at one of our café style tables. The relaxing ambiance of the granite and solid wood bar provides the perfect place for before and after drinks or your entire meal.

There is plenty of ample parking as well as a free municipal parking lot located just across the street. Valet car service is provided on Friday and Saturday nights. Delivery and take out is available Monday through Sunday during our regular business hours. Our catering rooms are just the right place for you to host your next special occasion or event.
